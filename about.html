In so few words

I study Computer Science and enjoy learning how systems work and how thoughtful design improves them. Outside of classes I build small tools, read, and write about ideas that connect technology and people.

I was born in Karachi, spent part of my childhood in Dubai, and later moved to Calgary. Moving often taught me to adapt easily and find comfort in change.

I’m curious about systems, design, and how small details shape structure. I like how lines of code can grow into patterns and patterns into systems. That curiosity led me to programming, testing, and refining until things feel right.

When I’m not coding, I read, learn about art and design, and think about how people build meaningful things.

Everything good is built slowly.
